Top Baseball Movies According to Blake

My top 3 Baseball movies of all tine.

Major League

This movie is so funny. Endless quoteable with a great soundtrack.

Moneyball

A thrilling true story that is also pretty funny. Character are amazing and leaving wondering what's next even if you already know the story.

For Love of the Game

Who ever has said there is no crying in baseball has never seen for the love of the game. It has something for everyone its funny at times its a love story and its baseball. Told over the course of a baseball game through flash backs but feels well put together at the same time.
